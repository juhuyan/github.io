About

I am a Doctor of Philosophy graduated from the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Waterloo. My research is focused on Deep learning, machine learning,and big data analysis facilitated transportation and pavement management advised by Susan Tighe. My research is supported by the MTO through the project collebration with CPATT. My Ph.D thesis is "Development of Machine Learning Based Analytical Tools for Pavement Performance Assessment and Crack Detection", and can be found on the UWSpace repository.
